Programme Curriculum

Three progressive parts. One complete cyber defender.

The 16-week programme builds from security foundations through offensive mastery to advanced autonomous defence — developing practical capability at every stage.

Part 1Weeks 1–5

Building the Foundations of Autonomous Defence

Strengthen core knowledge and build the essential skills to understand, defend, and secure modern systems in an AI-driven threat environment.

Introduction to Cybersecurity & Agentic AI

Understand the cybersecurity landscape and how Agentic AI is transforming defence, automation, and decision-making.

Cyber Laws & AI Governance

Learn the legal, ethical, and regulatory frameworks governing cybersecurity and the responsible use of AI.

Network Fundamentals

Build a strong understanding of networking concepts, protocols, and architectures that power secure communication.

System Hacking

Explore system-level attack vectors and techniques to understand how systems can be compromised from within.

Vulnerability Analysis

Learn to discover, assess, and prioritise vulnerabilities to strengthen systems and reduce organisational risk.

Part 2Weeks 6–10

Offensive Security & AI-Powered Threat Intelligence

Go beyond basics. Master advanced offensive techniques and leverage AI to detect, analyse, and outsmart modern threats before they materialise.

Network Scanning

Discover live hosts, open ports, and services using advanced scanning techniques and AI-assisted reconnaissance.

Spoofing & MITM Attacks

Manipulate packets and identities to bypass controls and perform man-in-the-middle and session hijacking attacks.

IDS / Firewall Evasion

Bypass and evade modern IDS/IPS and firewall systems using advanced obfuscation and tunnelling techniques.

Web Application Hacking

Identify and exploit vulnerabilities in web applications using industry-standard tools and AI-augmented testing.

Enumeration

Extract valuable information from systems, users, services, and infrastructure to build effective attack chains.

Mobile Security

Assess and exploit vulnerabilities in mobile applications and device communication channels.

Part 3Weeks 11–16

Advanced Autonomous Defence Systems

Master advanced techniques to detect, respond to, and neutralise sophisticated threats using AI-powered autonomous defence strategies at scale.

Malware Analysis

Analyse, reverse-engineer, and understand malware behaviour to build effective detection and mitigation strategies.

DoS / DDoS Defence

Design and implement robust strategies to detect, mitigate, and respond to denial-of-service attacks in real time.

Wireless Hacking

Assess and exploit wireless networks in controlled environments and learn to defend against wireless-based threats.

Social Engineering

Understand human psychology and attacker techniques used to manipulate and breach organisations.

Cloud Security

Secure cloud environments and applications using industry best practices and modern compliance frameworks.

IoT Security

Identify and secure IoT devices and networks from emerging vulnerabilities and infrastructure threats.
